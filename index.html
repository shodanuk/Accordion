Accordion. Pour Homme.

  • How do I start?

    Discuss the project with the client. Use the Project Orientation Questions as a guide to ensure everything is covered.

    If a written brief is supplied, double-check there are no gaps against the orientation questions.

    The aim is to gather information on:

    • Client background
    • Business & brand objectives
    • Website objectives
    • Target audience & high level requirements
    • Discuss Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), analytics and other measurements of success
    • Key dates & Budget
    • Key stakeholders
    • Technical considerations
    • What is required?

  • Who should be involved?

    Client Services and Planning are involved here with support from User Experience. View the specifics in the RASCI chart under the section “1. Orientation brief”
